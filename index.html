Shuteng Wang (王书腾)

I am a PhD student at the Max Planck Institute for Informatics, within the Visual Computing and Artificial Intelligence Department, and Saarland University. I am advised by Prof. Michael Möller and Dr. Vladislav Golyanik.

My research lies at the intersection of quantum machine learning and optimization. In particular, I focus on:

  • Circuit-enhanced AI models;
  • AI-driven circuit architecture search and synthesis;
  • Single- and few-shot quantum generative modeling;
  • (Quantum) Ising machines.
