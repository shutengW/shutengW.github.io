Shuteng Wang

I am a PhD student working on quantum machine learning, quantum optimization, and visual computing related problems. My research focuses on parametrized quantum circuits, quantum annealing, pruning and optimization, and quantum methods for learning compact and expressive representations.

More broadly, I am interested in the intersection of quantum computing and machine learning, especially in developing practically motivated methods for NISQ-era quantum models, combinatorial optimization for learning, and quantum approaches to geometric and visual inference.
